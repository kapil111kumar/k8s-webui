Help

  

    Things to take care of before writing instructions :

    -- Be specific about what you want to do. 

    -- Write the resource type before the resource name.

    -- Give space between special characters like ',' , '.' , '?' etc.

    -- Avoid giving full stop or commas in the sentence.



    Examples on how you can write the instructions

    * Create a pod:

        Please create pod with name mypod and image centos 

    * Create a deployment:

        Please create deployment with name myd1 and image centos and replicas 5 

        or

        Create a deployment with name myd1 and image centos 
 
    * Scale your replicas:

        Scale my deployment myd1 to 10 replicas 

        or

        Increase to no of pods in myd1 to 10


    * Expose your services:

        Expose my deployment myd1 with port 80

    * Describe pods: 

        Describe about pod mypod
 
    * Desribe deployments:

        Describe about deployment myd1
 
    * Desribe services:

        Describe about service mysvc
 
    * Delete any pods: 

        Remove pod mypod

        or

        Delete pod mypod

    * Delete any deployments:

        Remove deployment myd1

    * Delete any services:

        Delete service mysvs

    * Delete complete environment created:

        Delete everything

         or

        Delete all resources
